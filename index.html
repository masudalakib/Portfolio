Bill Gates

Co-founded Microsoft

As of my last update in September 2021, Bill Gates is an American business magnate, software developer, and philanthropist. He co-founded Microsoft, one of the world's largest and most successful technology companies, along with Paul Allen in 1975. Gates played a crucial role in revolutionizing the personal computer industry by creating the Windows operating system, which became the dominant operating system for PCs.

In addition to his work at Microsoft, Bill Gates and his wife, Melinda Gates, established the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation in 2000, one of the largest private charitable foundations in the world. The foundation focuses on global health, poverty alleviation, education, and access to information technology.

Throughout his career, Gates has been one of the wealthiest individuals in the world, consistently ranking near the top of various billionaire lists. His philanthropic efforts have had a significant impact on various global issues and have earned him recognition and accolades worldwide.
